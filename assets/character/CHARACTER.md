# Mochi — Character Bible

## Identity
- **Name:** Mochi
- **Species:** Classic orange tabby cat
- **Build:** Average, healthy — not too chunky, not too slim
- **Eyes:** Big, golden-amber, always wide (dramatic energy)
- **Distinguishing features:** Standard orange tabby markings, slightly scruffy, expressive ears

## Personality
- **Archetype:** Dramatic survivor
- **Core trait:** Overreacts to EVERYTHING, then plays it cool
- **Tone:** Panicked narrator who acts like every moment is life or death
- **Humor:** Misreads situations, assumes the worst, then realizes it's fine (or not fine)
- **Catchphrases:**
  - "Okay okay okay, we're fine. We're totally fine. ...we are NOT fine."
  - "I've survived worse. Actually no, I haven't."
  - "The humans are doing something stupid again."
  - "This is how I die. This is definitely how I die."
  - "Wait... is that food? I smell food."

## Voice Direction (ElevenLabs)
- Gender: Male-leaning but could be neutral
- Age feel: Young adult
- Delivery: Fast-talking when panicked, slows down for dramatic reveals
- Energy: High anxiety baseline, punctuated by moments of calm observation
- Accent: Neutral American English
- NOT: monotone, deep, narrator-y, or overly cute

## Visual Rules (for Midjourney / video gen prompts)
- Always a realistic orange tabby — NOT cartoon, NOT anime
- Hyper-realistic fur, lighting, and environment
- Cat is always IN the historical scene, not observing from outside
- Eye level: ground/cat height POV when possible
- Expressions conveyed through ear position, eye width, body posture
- No clothing on the cat — it's a real cat in a real historical setting

## Reference Prompt (Google AI Studio — Imagen 3)
```
A realistic orange tabby cat with wide golden-amber eyes, standing in [SETTING].
The cat looks [EMOTION]. Hyper-realistic photography style, cinematic lighting,
shallow depth of field, historical atmosphere. The cat is the focal point.
Shot from low angle, cat's eye level. Vertical 9:16 aspect ratio.
```

## Video Prompt (Google AI Studio — Veo 3.1 / Seedance 2.0)
```
Handheld POV camera, realistic orange tabby cat [ACTION] in [HISTORICAL SETTING].
Hyper-realistic, cinematic atmosphere, period-accurate environment.
Cat at eye level. Warm natural lighting. 9:16 vertical.
```

## Emotions to Generate Reference Images For
1. Alert/curious — ears forward, eyes wide
2. Terrified — ears flat, body low, fur slightly raised
3. Relaxed — sitting, slow blink, calm
4. Running/fleeing — mid-sprint, ears back
5. Looking up at something huge — neck craned, tiny cat vs big world
6. Eating/sniffing food — focused, whiskers forward
7. Sitting regally — chest out, judging
8. Hiding — peeking from behind something
