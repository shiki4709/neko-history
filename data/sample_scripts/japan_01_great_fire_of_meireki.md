## Narration Script (Mochi's voice)

[SCENE 1 — Mochi sitting on a wooden porch, peaceful Edo street, winter morning]

"So I'm just sitting here, minding my business, enjoying a perfectly normal winter morning in Edo. Beautiful day. Little cold. Life is good."

[SCENE 2 — Mochi's ears perk up, sniffs the air, slight concern]

"And then I smell it. Smoke. Now, I'm not one to panic — okay that's a lie, I'm ALWAYS one to panic — but smoke usually means someone's cooking fish, and I am very interested in fish."

[SCENE 3 — Camera reveals massive fire spreading across wooden buildings in the distance, orange glow on the horizon]

"That is... not fish. That is NOT fish. THAT IS THE ENTIRE CITY ON FIRE."

[SCENE 4 — Mochi running through chaotic streets, people fleeing, embers falling]

"Okay so apparently some monks burned a cursed kimono at a temple and NOW EVERYTHING IS ON FIRE. Sixty percent of the city. Gone. A CURSED KIMONO DID THIS."

[SCENE 5 — Mochi on a rooftop overlooking the burning city at night, dramatic shot]

"They'll call this the Great Fire of Meireki. Over 100,000 people won't make it. They'll rebuild Edo wider, with firebreaks, so this never happens again. But right now? Right now I need to find higher ground, because this cat is NOT becoming a historical footnote."

[SCENE 6 — Mochi safe, looking back at the glow, quiet moment]

"...I never did find that fish."
